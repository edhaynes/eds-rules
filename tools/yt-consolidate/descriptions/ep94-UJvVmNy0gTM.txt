Episode 94 of Ed's Rules. After the conversation with Guy Turgeon (a chief architect at Red Hat, Telco vertical), Guy took a scalpel to the 100 rules — cutting them into a layered structure: a small immutable axiom core plus composable preference layers (personal/architect, project, employer). This episode walks the buckets — axioms, engineering discipline, quality, working with humans & agents — and the cache-hierarchy idea behind slicing rules across a small-model crew.
