Episode 93 of Ed's 100 Rules — the rule-quality series. Ed Haynes sits down with Guy Turgeon to grade the rule set: where the 100 Rules came from (Colin Powell's 90% rule), what generalizes versus what's just Ed's own, API design as the single source of truth, attack surface and container security (UBI, daemonless containers), and lessons from a career in embedded software (VxWorks, real-time systems).
